HISTORY OF PHOTOGRAPHY

FAMOUS PHOTOGRAPHERS

Tina Modotti

Tina Modotti was an Italian photographer, model, and actress. She was born on August 17,1896 in Udine, Italy and died on January 5, 1942 in Mexcio City, Mexcio.




Gerda Taro

Gerda Taro was a war photographer, the companion and professional partner of photographer Robert Capa. Taro is regarded as the first female photojournalist to cover the front lines of a war and to die while doing so. She was born on August 1, 1910 in Stuttgart, Germany and she died on July 26, 1937 in El Escorial, Spain
